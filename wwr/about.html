The Rafting Company

Our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences across Nigeria’s beautiful rivers—from the Osun River to the mighty Niger. We aim to inspire a passion for nature and promote eco-tourism through adventure.

History

Founded in 2010 in Jos, Plateau State, The Rafting Company began with a simple goal: to bring adventure tourism to Nigeria. Over the past decade, we’ve grown to become the country’s leading rafting outfitter, known for our expert guides, commitment to safety, and love for the outdoors.
