Overview

purpose

The purpose of establishing rafting online presence for a rafting business is to leverage digital channels to attract, engage, and convert potential customers, thereby growing the business and enhancing its overall success in the adventure tourism industry.

Audience

Our target audience consists of adventure enthusiasts aged 18-45 who seek thrilling outdoor experiences like water rafting. They are active, health-conscious, and value new and exciting activities. Predominantly urban and suburban dwellers with middle to high incomes, they are often college-educated and engaged with social media platforms. They prefer experiential purchases over material goods, influenced by peer reviews and social media. They appreciate detailed information on safety, enjoy engaging with adventure stories and community content, and look for easy booking options and customer support. They are drawn to high-quality visuals, interactive elements, and special offers for their adventure activities.
